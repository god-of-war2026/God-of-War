## 📖 About God of War

**God of War** is an award-winning action-adventure game developed by Santa Monica Studio. The game follows **Kratos**, the former Greek God of War, as he begins a new life in the realm of Norse gods and monsters alongside his son, Atreus.

Set in a beautifully crafted world inspired by Norse mythology, players encounter legendary creatures, powerful enemies, and ancient mysteries while experiencing a deeply personal story about family, redemption, and destiny.
